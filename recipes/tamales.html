Tamales de frijol

This delicious food is a staple in the mexican suisine. My mother makes this food often as it is one of my favorite dishs.

Be warned though! Making this dish is a lengthy process, but the hard work is rewarded ten fold!

Ingredients
  • Beans
  • Cheese
  • Masa
  • Corn Husks
Instructions
  1. First you will need to prepare the masa with the instructions given on the package
  2. Then spread the masa over the corn husk
  3. Spread beans over the masa and add a block of cheese
