Unos sopes bien perrones

This delicious food is a staple in the mexican cuisine. My mother made this food often as it was my favorite dish.

At the day of writing this I ate some delicous sopes. She put beans, sour cream, cheese, longaniza, and topped it with lettuce.

Ingredients
  • Beans
  • Cheese
  • Masa
  • longaniza
  • Sour Cream
  • lettuce
Instructions
  1. First you will need to prepare the masa with the instructions given on the package
  2. Then you will make the masa into the shape of a sope and put it on a skillet
  3. After it's done cooking put beans on it and put toppings on
